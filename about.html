About Me

My Story

Born in Brazil, I always dreamed of living abroad and building something that connected creativity with the wider world.

At 17, I moved to Toronto and began studying Graphic Design at Humber Polytechnic. Over three years of meeting inspiring people, immersing myself in the city's art and design scene, and learning from exceptional professors, I found the two areas of design that truly resonate with me: editorial and marketing.

Today, I create clean, modern, and refined visuals designed to make brands feel memorable and impossible to ignore.

My Approach

I design for the feeling a composition leaves behind.

Every project starts with understanding: the brand, the audience, and the emotional response a piece of design needs to create.

My Brazilian roots inform how I think about color, rhythm, and warmth, while my Toronto education has sharpened my sense of editorial structure and modern aesthetics.

I'm fluent in Portuguese and English — and comfortable working across print, digital, and social media contexts.
